Baking with Angela

Baking has always been a serious passion of mine. I've always enjoyed sharing my favorite recipes, tips and tricks- and contrary to popular belief, you don't need any magic to make your sweet treats delicious. All you need is the right recipe and patience!

One of my favorite treats are homemade chocolate chip cookies. They're easy to make and packed with sweet nostalgia.

Chocolate Chip Cookies, here we come!

Amount Ingredient
226g Butter
100g Sugar
200g Brown Sugar
2 each Eggs
380g All Purpose Flour
2 tsp Cornstarch
1 tsp Baking Soda
1 tsp Baking Powder
200g Chocolate Chips
  1. Cube the butter and let it come to room temperature.
  2. Cream butter and all sugars together for 5 minutes or until the mixture becomes a bit lighter in color.
  3. Add eggs to mix and combine.
  4. Combine all dry goods together first, then all at once into the mix.
  5. Add your Chocolate Chips and mix until just homogenous.
  6. Scoop and bake @ 325F/160C.

  7. and remember to share!
